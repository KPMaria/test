Our Mission

Outdoor Adventure is a premier adventure activity service provider specializing in curated human-powered adventure activities from trekking, peak climbing, adventure courses, and mountain expeditions. If you are overwhelmed and worried about all the logistics, safety, and planning then we have a solution for you.

Our teams are based in London and Kathmandu. We aim to provide the best adventure trips around the globe. our staff members and clients are working for the highest quality expedition and commitment to sustainable practices to protect and create a better environment in these beautiful pristine destinations.

Our Core Values

-Customer safety first, well-being, and enjoyment of adventure tours are our utmost priority.

-Commitment to ethical and sustainability ideas.

-Driving change that benefits the society and environment.

-Creating unique and enjoyable experiences while exploring new adventure destinations.
